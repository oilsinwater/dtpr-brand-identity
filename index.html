What this is. An inventory of the DTPR visual identity as it exists in the wild — across dtpr.io, vision.dtpr.io, makeshift2026.dtpr.io, docs.dtpr.io, the Assembly Day deck, the Message Map, and MakeShift print assets — with screenshots, source-code citations, and analysis. What it is not. A prescriptive brand book. Decisions and color-token lockups are observed or inferred, never invented. Every open question is named; nothing is silently resolved.

How to read this audit

Twelve pages, organized from the meta-question (where did the brand come from?) through each surface (per-site breakdowns) to the visual building blocks (marks, color, type, icons, illustration, voice) to a final findings page that names what's load-bearing and what isn't.

Tags used throughout

OBSERVED INFERRED PROPOSED MEMO OPEN vision.dtpr.io dtpr.io makeshift docs.dtpr.io Assembly Day

OBSERVED = empirically present in source code or visible on a live page. INFERRED = derived from how the assets are deployed, not from a written rule. PROPOSED = recommended direction, not yet decided. MEMO = sourced from the brand foundation memo (v2), not from a live surface — epistemic distinction maintained. OPEN = a decision the brand book has to surface for the team.
