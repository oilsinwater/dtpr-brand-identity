Sprint Context & Rationale:

  1. Why a second box? The first box mapped static visual form. This second box maps dynamic translation and environmental constraints (how the visual elements morph when responding to screen size, user interaction, live data feeds, or domain transitions).
  2. Exhaustive parameter combinations: The six parameters defined here (Grid Decomposition, Scale Adaptation, Interaction, Dynamic Overlay, Gateway Hand-off, and Nav Anchors) govern the interactive grammar of the system.
  3. Synthesis with Form Box: Combine your chosen Form recipe (from chapter 13) with a Process recipe from this table (e.g. "B-C-C-A-B-B") to lock down the exact structural behaviors for the consolidated digital properties.
